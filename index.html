Who We Are

More Than A Conference.
A Movement.

Council of Diplomatic Affairs is a Model United Nations conference built for a generation that doesn't just talk about change — they draft it.

We blend the rigor of traditional UN diplomacy with a Gen Z energy that keeps things real, relevant, and razor sharp. Whether you're a first-timer finding your voice or a veteran with a binder full of position papers — this is your room.

Our committees are curated with precision, our executive board comprises seasoned MUNers, and our delegations are a vibrant mix of leaders, debaters, and thinkers who refuse to be boring about the world's biggest problems.
